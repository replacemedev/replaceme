Active

location_on Remote schedule Full-Time payments $800/mo

Job Description

We are seeking a highly creative and detail-oriented Thumbnail Designer to join our dynamic media team. In this role, you will be responsible for conceptualizing and designing eye-catching, high-converting thumbnails for our YouTube channels and social media platforms.

The ideal candidate possesses a deep understanding of visual storytelling, color theory, and current digital media trends to maximize click-through rates (CTR) and audience engagement.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design compelling and clickable thumbnails for video content across multiple platforms.
  • Analyze performance metrics to A/B test and optimize design strategies.
  • Collaborate closely with video editors and content strategists to ensure visual consistency.
  • Stay up-to-date with design trends, platform algorithms, and competitor strategies.

Requirements & Skills

Required Skills

Adobe Photoshop Graphic Design Color Theory A/B Testing Typography

Experience & Education

  • Minimum 2 years of experience in digital design, specifically focused on social media thumbnails.
  • A strong portfolio demonstrating high-CTR thumbnail designs.
  • Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite, especially Photoshop and Illustrator.
