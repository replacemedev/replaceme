Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about how Replace Me works.

What is Replace Me?

add

Replace Me is a direct hiring platform that connects businesses with Filipino virtual assistants and remote workers. Employers can hire and manage talent directly, with no agency fees, salary markups, or ongoing commissions.

Can I unsubscribe after hiring?

add

Yes, you can unsubscribe at anytime! There are no contracts, so once you’ve hired someone, you can cancel your subscription and restart it later if you need to find another worker.

Do I pay monthly fees for my worker?

add

Nope! Since we’re not an agency, you hire and pay your remote worker directly (with no on-going fees!). Even if you unsubscribe, you still have complete control and management over your hire.

What is the refund policy?

add

We have a 30-day money back guarantee! So if for any reason you are not 100% satisfied or don’t find the perfect match within 30 days, we will give you a full refund.

What can I do with a FREE account?

add

You can post job listings and review applications to get an idea of potential candidates. However, you won’t be able to message and hire them until you subscribe to a paid account.
